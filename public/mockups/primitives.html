Layout Principles

Proximity
Group related items tightly (8px gap). Separate distinct groups with more space (16–20px).
Hierarchy
One clear headline per card. Font-size steps: 18px title → 14px body → 12px secondary → 11px label.
Alignment
Left-align text. Right-align numbers in tables. Keep a consistent left edge.
Density
Prefer compact, information-dense layouts. Space is for separation, not for filling area.
Composition
Compose freely from patterns. A card might have person lockup + stat row + severity block + data rows — whatever best answers the question.
