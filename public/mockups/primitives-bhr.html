Layout Principles

Proximity
Group related items tightly (8px gap). Separate distinct groups with more space (16-20px).
Hierarchy
Fields for headlines, Inter for body. Size steps: 18px title, 14px body, 12px secondary, 11px label.
Alignment
Left-align text. Right-align numbers in tables. Keep a consistent left edge.
Density
Prefer compact, information-dense layouts. Space is for separation, not for filling area.
Color Restraint
Green is the only brand color. Warm neutrals for text/borders. Status colors (red, orange, blue) only in badge pills.
